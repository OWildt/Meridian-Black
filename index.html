The Universe (Plain Language)

“After AI” (AAI) counts from the year general artificial intelligence became basic infrastructure. In 378 AAI, vessel technology—long-range living ships and remote bodies—moved commerce and culture off‑world. Earth quieted but endured.

Today, cities run on light and ledger. Fashion is firmware: textiles negotiate privacy; jewelry carries encrypted keys; scent can unlock a stored memory. Art and devices are co-authored by makers and models; ownership is tracked on public ledgers; identity is verified by hash.

Entrepreneurship on Old Earth faded while vessels took the spotlight. Meridian Black celebrates those who stayed—makers and founders working with real materials in real gravity.

Founded 379 AAI

House Backstory

During a city power‑shed along the prime meridian, a circle of Earth‑based patrons staged a private sale. Lots were human‑made with verifiable custody; bids were settled on a cold, audited ledger. The success—and the mood of that night—became the model.

Incorporated as Meridian Black, the house restores a high‑standards marketplace to Old Earth: glamorous, discreet, rigorous. Our symbol—a thin meridian through a dark circle—marks our gavel, plinth plaques, and encrypted seals of sale.
